23 求職 Job Hunting

  • Go to
  • Meaning
  • Reading
  • Context
  • Kanji Composition
  • Progress

Meaning

Primary

Job Hunting

Word Type

noun, する verb, の adjective

Explanation

Ugh, time to request employment again. Time to go job hunting.

Note

Reading

きゅうしょく

Explanation

This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.

Note

Context

Context Sentences

失業なんて気にするなよ。俺なんてかれこれもう約二年間も求職活動中だぜ。

Don’t worry about being unemployed. I’ve been job hunting for almost two years now.
