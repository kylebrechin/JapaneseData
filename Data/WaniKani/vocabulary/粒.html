44 粒 Grain

  • Go to
  • Meaning
  • Reading
  • Context
  • Kanji Composition
  • Progress

Meaning

Primary

Grain

Alternatives

Bead, Counter For Grains

Word Type

counter, independent noun

Explanation

Grains are made of single grains. You can also use this to refer to a bead or use it as a counter for grains.

Note

Reading

つぶ

Explanation

You take all the grain and put it in a tube (つぶ). Just imagine doing this, grain by grain.

Note

Context

Patterns of Use

Common Word Combinations

粒の形

the shape of the grain

粒の数

the number of grains

粒の色

the color of the grain

Context Sentences

私は一粒の岩塩をオブラートで包みました。

I wrapped a single grain of rock salt in a wafer.
