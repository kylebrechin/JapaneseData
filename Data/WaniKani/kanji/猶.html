56 猶 Still

  • Go to
  • Meaning
  • Reading
  • Found In Vocabulary

Radical Combination

  • 犭 Animal
  • Horns
  • 酉 Alcohol

Meaning

Primary

Still

Alternative

Yet

Mnemonic

An animal with horns is drinking our alcohol? Still?

Hints

You keep checking outside to see if they have stopped, but they are still drinking. They haven't stopped yet.

Note

Readings

On’yomi

ゆう, ゆ

Kun’yomi

なお

Nanori

None

Mnemonic

The animal with horns that is still drinking your alcohol is no animal at all. It is actually one of the local youths (ゆう) dressed up like a horned animal so they can drink as long as they want and you'll be too scared to shoo them away.

Hints

You keep checking, but that youth is still drinking. Of course, you don't know they are a youth, so it's quite impressive an animal can drink this much.
