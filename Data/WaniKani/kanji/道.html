8 道 Road

  • Go to
  • Meaning
  • Reading
  • Found In Vocabulary
  • Progress

Radical Combination

  • Scooter
  • 首 Neck

Meaning

Primary

Road

Alternatives

Street, Path, Way

Mnemonic

Aim the scooter's neck at the road! Scooters are only meant for driving on streets and paths, and if you go the wrong way, you'll probably break your own neck!

Hints

Imagine riding your scooter down the road. You're carefully aiming the scooter's neck the right way so you don't end up going out of control and dying.

Note

Readings

On’yomi

どう

Kun’yomi

みち

Nanori

None

Mnemonic

To remember the reading どう we use the word "doughnut."

You're traveling down this road so fast that you don't see the giant doughnut (どう) that's rolling toward you until the last minute. You crash into it, and both you and the doughnut go tumbling down. Ahg, your neck!

Hints

Alternate ending: You do a sweet jump, go right through the doughnut hole, take a bite, and land safely back onto the road!
