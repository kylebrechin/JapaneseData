Radical Combination

  • 牛 Cow
  • 勿 Wing

Meaning

Primary

Thing

Mnemonic

A winged cow is flying in the air. What a strange thing! You’ve heard of flying pigs before, but cows? This is a new and strange thing indeed! Cows with wings! What a silly thing to imagine.

Hints

Imagine if cows could fly. Wouldn’t you be jealous of them? I mean, if a cow can fly, any old thing should be able to, right?

Note

Readings

On’yomi

ぶつ, もつ

Kun’yomi

もの

Nanori

None

Mnemonic

You want a thing to help you fly with the cow so you put on your flying boots (ぶつ). Your flying boots are a pretty strange thing in themselves, but they're the only way you can keep up.

Hints

Imagine a thing like flying boots. Do you believe such a thing could be real?
