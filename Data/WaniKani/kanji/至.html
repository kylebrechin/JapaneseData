Radical Combination

  • 至 Mole

Meaning

Primary

Attain

Mnemonic

Dig up a mole and you attain a mole status. You're always trying to attain a new level for yourself and now you get to be on the mole level!

Hints

It's actually pretty easy to attain mole status. Just stand in a field. Wait for the ground to move under you and shove your hand in the earth. Blam! An achievement pops up above your head and you're there.

Note

Mnemonic

Mole isn't enough, you want to attain sheep (し) status next. Everyone knows it goes human > mole> sheep. Now you need to stand in the field with the mole and get a sheep to come over.

Hints

If you can attain two animal levels in one day you'll be a legend on the leaderboards.
